Overview

Destinations Covered :  Delhi – Agra – Delhi
Tour Duration :  1 Day
Treasure Trip India offers you the same Day Agra Tour by Car in which you get the opportunity to explore the heritage of Agra in the shortest time possible. The journey starts from the National Capital of India, and ends at the same place covering a short journey to Taj Mahal, Agra Fort and Baby Taj in Agra.

  1. Day 1 : 06:00 AM – Pick up from Delhi
    Early morning, your driver will pick you up from Indira Gandhi International Airport or your hotel in Delhi. From here, we shall drive towards Agra through Yamuna Expressway and will reach Agra in less than three hours. Upon arrival to Agra, head straight to the Taj Mahal.
  2. Day 1 : 09:00 AM – Taj Mahal Sightseeing
    Taj Mahal is a beautiful monument located on the banks of River Yamuna. The monument was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaj Mahal who died while giving birth to their 14 th child. It is believed that more than 25,000 people worked for 22 years to build this monument. It is built with white marble bought from Makrana, a town in Rajasthan.

    Taj Mahal comprises of a center tomb with four minarets on each side. The graves of Shah Jahan and Mumtaj Mahal are located at the center of the tomb. Due to its beautiful architecture, the monument is listed among the Seven Wonders of the World, and to celebrate the love of the Emperor and his wife, the monument is also called the Symbol of love.
    Note: Taj Mahal remain closed on Friday.
  3. Day 1 : 11:30 AM – Itmad-ud-Daula’s Tomb
    Known as the Baby Taj, a replica of the Taj Mahal, is the tomb of Itmad-Ud-Daula, which is famous for its historical value and grandeur. You will also be fascinated by the amazing architecture of this monument.
  4. Day 1 : 01:00 PM – Lunch
    We will enter a restaurant/hotel to enjoy a healthy lunch. Here, you are allowed to spend some time in leisure. The journey resumes post lunch.
  5. Day 1 : 02:30 PM – Agra Fort
    Next destination is the Agra Fort, popularly known as the sister monument of the Taj Mahal. Agra Fort was built by Mughal Emperor Akbar as the capital of his empire. It also served as the residence of the royal family. Post-independence, the fort was transformed into a royal museum. You may find innumerable belongings of the erstwhile empire, including their costumes, jewelry and armory.
  6. Day 1 : 04:00 PM – Shopping
    If time permits, you are allowed to wander in the streets of Agra to collect some souvenirs. Agra is globally famous for Inlayed Marble ,leather-ware, brass-ware, carpets, jewellery and embroidery work.
  7. Day 1 : 05:00 PM – Departure
    Get back into the car to drive back to Delhi. Upon arrival to Delhi, your driver will transfer you to your hotel or Indira Gandhi international Airport. The Same Day Agra Tour by Car ends here.
    Treasure Trip India wishes you the best for your journeys ahead.

NOTE:  The timings of the above itinerary are flexible; hence you can change the timings of your itinerary as per your own convenience.
